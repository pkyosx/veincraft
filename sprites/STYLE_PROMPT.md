# Tiny Swords Style Prompt (by Gemini analysis)

Use this as the style anchor for ALL character/asset generation.

## Full Prompt

Generate art in the following style:

Top-down isometric 16-bit era pixel art with a clean, vector-like finish and crisp, aliased lines. Outline style is consistent dark blue-black, exactly 1-pixel wide, with zero anti-aliasing. Proportions are classic chibi/SD: large heads (1:2 ratio), squat bodies, short limbs, and compact, rounded silhouettes. Shading is simple, hard-edged cel-shading with strictly 2 to 3 distinct tones per color area (base + shadow + optional highlight). Lighting is top-down. The color palette is cool and slightly muted. Metal/Armor is desaturated gray-blue (#BDCED3, #A5CDE1) with dark cyan outlines. Skin is peachy-tan (#FBC9A7, #E19F78). Wood is warm gold-brown (#D6B97A) with red-brown accents. Faction blue is deep cobalt (#3F578F). Details are simplified; faces use 2-pixel dots for eyes and simple lines for hair, prioritizing readability over complexity. Pixel density is consistent, with characters approximately 32x32 to 48x48 pixels. Characters are drawn from a 3/4 top-down perspective, facing diagonal-forward. Idle animations are simple bobbing; walking frames alternate leg positions without major silhouette changes. The style is defined by its clean, non-dithered gradients and cute, stout character models. NEVER use noisy textures, soft airbrushing, dithered shading, realistic proportions, or photoreal colors. Environment art must follow the same clean pixel-grid aesthetic, featuring segmented cliff-edges, simple green tile maps, conical simplified trees, and clean-lined buildings with saturated blue roofs and textured straw.

- Dithered shading
- Realistic proportions
- Photoreal colors
