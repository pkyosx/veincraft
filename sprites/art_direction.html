Style Rules

DO

  • Thick dark outlines (near-black blue #000020)
  • Chibi proportions (big head, stubby limbs)
  • Muted earthy tones, occasional bright accents
  • Readable silhouettes at small sizes
  • Subtle animations (sway, bounce, ripple)
  • Soft 2-3 tone shading
  • 3/4 isometric view angle

DON'T

  • Pure black #000000 outlines (use blue-black)
  • Neon / saturated colors (except UI)
  • Too much detail at 64px (readability first)
  • Mix realistic + chibi art styles
  • Thin 1px outlines (too fragile)
  • Harsh gradients or photo textures
  • Characters larger than 70% of frame
